Veggie Pizza Recipe

Veggies are very good. PRO TIP Mix Veggies with unhealthy foods.


Ingredients You Need:

  • 2 Pack of Mushroom I love mushroom
  • 3 pack of spinach I love spinach.
  • 2 Tomato Paste

Recipe to Follow

  1. Wash your Spinach and Mushroom
  2. Cut the Mushroom
  3. Make the Dough and put tomato paste on it
  4. Put in the oven after putting Spinach and Mushroom as the top
